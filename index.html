Who We Are

Bowhead is an India focused long-only boutique investment firm, identifying opportunities across the market capitalization curve with a long-term view. We run a concentrated portfolio of high-conviction stocks, selected based on rigorous research through proprietary models.

Behavioral Edge

We invest in non-consensus ideas. We are not shy of being early investors, are not constrained by benchmark-based allocation, understand long term market cycles, and hold despite volatility.

Differentiated stock selection process

We do a deep dive in only those stocks/sectors where street is likely to be misinformed, ignorant or behind the curve.

Proprietary frameworks and processes

We are a process-oriented team and pride ourselves in having detailed frameworks developed internally over the years. Each of our frameworks is implemented via well thought out processes for efficient output.

Our Offerings

Investment Advisory (IA)

Bowhead Investment Advisors Private Limited is a SEBI-registered Investment Advisor engaged in providing non-binding investment advisory services in connection with investments primarily in listed equity.

Alternative Investment Fund (AIF)

Bowhead Focussed Fund is a scheme of Bowhead Trust which is registered with the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) as an open-ended Category III Alternative Investment Fund.
